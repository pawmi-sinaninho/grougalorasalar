## Evidence hierarchy

1. Direct current-version observation with before/after state and exact action.
2. Two independent detailed sources agreeing.
3. One detailed source.
4. Screenshot inference.
5. Generic DOFUS convention.
6. Hypothesis.

Generic game convention alone may never decide a critical rule.
